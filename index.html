Create a new group
Groups are places where you chat about various topics with members of a team.
To get started, pick a name for your new group (e.g. My Company). The name can be changed later by you or any other member of the group.
Join someone's group
Connect with another user to see the groups that they have added you to.
If someone has asked you to chat with them, enter their Blockstack ID (e.g. johndoe.id.blockstack) to see their groups.
